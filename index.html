Privacy Policy

Keel  ·  Effective May 3, 2026  ·  Keel App

Short version: Keel reads your weight data from Apple Health, processes everything on your device, and never sends any data anywhere. No accounts. No servers. No ads.

What Keel does

Keel is a weight-trend app that reads body weight samples from Apple Health, applies a 7-day smoothing average and linear regression, and displays your trend on a chart — entirely on your device.

Data we access

With your permission, Keel reads body weight samples from Apple HealthKit. This is the only data the app requests. Keel requests read-only access; it never writes to or modifies your Health data.

You grant or revoke this permission at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Keel. If permission is revoked, the app will have no data to display.

How your data is used

All computation happens locally on your device. Specifically:

  • Weight samples are read into memory to calculate a rolling average and trend line.
  • No data is transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.
  • No data is stored outside of Apple Health — Keel holds values in memory only while the app is open.

HealthKit data — what we will never do

In accordance with Apple's HealthKit guidelines and our commitment to you, health and fitness data obtained through HealthKit will never be:

  • Sold to advertisers, data brokers, or any third party
  • Used for advertising or marketing purposes
  • Disclosed to third parties without your explicit consent
  • Used for any purpose other than providing the app's core features

Purchases

Keel may offer optional purchases processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit. We never receive or store your payment information. Purchase history is managed by Apple and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Crash reports

If you have opted into sharing diagnostics with Apple (a device-level setting), Apple may provide us with anonymised crash logs through App Store Connect. These logs contain technical information about the crash (stack trace, iOS version, device model) and no Health data. This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy, not this one.

A future version of Keel may include a dedicated crash-reporting SDK. If that happens, this policy will be updated before the update ships, and you will be notified in the App Store release notes.

Children's privacy

Keel is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page and note the change in the App Store release notes for that version. Continued use of the app after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
